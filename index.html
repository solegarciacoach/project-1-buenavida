Our Story

The heart of Buenavida

We're Coni and Sole, and Buenavida started from our own paths to wellness. Coni battled the crushing pressure of burnout and found solace in the mind-body connection through movement and mindfulness. Sole's travels opened her eyes to the power of traditional wellness practices and inspired her to bring that vibrancy back home to Spain.

Together, we realized a shared truth: true wellbeing isn't one-size-fits-all. It's a personal journey made of everyday choices. So, we created Buenavida, meaning "good life", as a warm, welcoming space for everyone to explore their own path.

We offer workshops on stress-relief to nutrition, a blog filled with simple tips, and a supportive community. We believe everyone deserves to feel their best, and Buena Vida is here to guide you.
